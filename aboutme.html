Top 2 Sephora Perfumes

Kayali Vanilla 28 is a brown sugar vanilla perfume. It's boozy, sweet and intoxicating. It's easily layerable as it smells like vanilla extract with more depth. The travel size is $29, the 50ml is $100 and the 100ml is $138.

Commodity Milk- is a slightly woody marshmallow fragrance. It's my favorite out of the Milk collection because its not too woody or burnt, it's fluffy, lactonic and airy. This might actually be my favorite fragrance of all time at this moment. The travel size is $32 and the full size is $150.
