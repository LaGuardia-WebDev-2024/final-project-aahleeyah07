Aaliyah's Perfume Obsession

I have a fragrance/perfume addiction. I love finding new things to smell like, new things that smell like things I've always wanted to smell like and just things to smell. I currently want to smell like a croissant. I have recently only been buying samples because blind buying is bad. Never blind buy. I like to review perfumes because there are alot of people that lie because they're getting paid. I dont operate like that. I love perfumes and you should too. Here's 6 perfumes you should have in your collection.

I like vanilla and I like marshmallow. So those are the only fragrances on this list. I do like other things and I have other things in my collection but those are my favorites. I like to smell like food. I loveeee gourmand perfumes. Im on the hunt to find summer gourmands because you can't wear certain things all year round! In the background I wanted to smell like a strawberry shortcake but it ended up being cake with strawberry on the side. But you should try it anyway because it smells great.
