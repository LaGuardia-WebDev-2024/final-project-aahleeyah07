Top 2 Indie Perfumes

Dapper Fragrances Escapade Gourmande is a dupe for Maison Mataha Escapade Gourmande. While it's always good to buy the actual fragrance, unfortunately Escapade Gourmande doesnt last on my skin even though it's $215. Dapper's version is a little sweeter i'd say but also still keeps the DNA. I love this dupe. The 5ml is $15, the 15ml is $25, the 20ml is $30, the 30ml is $35, the 50ml is $65 and the 100ml is $120.

Chantel's Whipped Marshmallow Cake is a very affordable fragrance from SugarMeSweet on Etsy. It's a collab with Sweet Smells with Chantel on TikTok and smells exactly like the name. It also lasts atleast 6-8 hours on my skin. When it dries down it smells just like Delizia di Marshmallow. It's not headache inducing either. So so good. The 10ml is $12, the 30ml is $29, the 50ml is $42 and the 100ml is $78.
