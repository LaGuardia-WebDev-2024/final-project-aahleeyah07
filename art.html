Top 2 Arabic Perfumes

Al Rehab Choco Musk is a very very affordable fragrance at around $5-$7 on Amazon. I prefer to have the oil and spray version because the oil lasts all day and is strong but can't be put on clothes. The spray lasts on clothes but not skin. Layering them creates a masterpiece. It smells like vanilla to some, but to me it smells like chocolate chip cookies.

Lattafa Angham is a dupe for Burberry Goddess. It's priced at around $33-$35 and way better than Goddess in my opinion. It's more vanilla heavy while still keeping that Goddess DNA whereas Goddess is more lavender heavy. It's Burberry Goddess but sweeter. Not too sweet though!
